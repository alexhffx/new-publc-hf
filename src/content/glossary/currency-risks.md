[**Currency Risks**](https://www.hedgeflows.com/fx-risk-management) are an aggregate of the potential risk of losses from fluctuating foreign exchange rates when a company has currency exposure from its business activities. Currency Risks are often separated into Transactional, Translational, and Economic risks depending on areas affected by currency exposure.
