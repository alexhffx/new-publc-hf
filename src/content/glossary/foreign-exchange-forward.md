**Currency forward or foreign exchange (FX) forward** is a bilateral contract in the FX market that locks in the exchange rate for the purchase or sale of a currency on a future date. Currency forwards are primary tools used to hedge FX risks that can be customised to meet specific payment schedules matching underlying commercial transactions.
