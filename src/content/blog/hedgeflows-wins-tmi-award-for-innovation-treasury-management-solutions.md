We, at HedgeFlows are delighted to announce that our dedication to innovation and excellence has been recognised with the prestigious TMI Award for Innovation in Treasury Management Solutions.

## TMI AWARDS

[The TMI Awards for Innovation](https://treasury-management.com/awards/) serve as a beacon of excellence in the treasury profession, celebrating organisations that push the boundaries of innovation and deliver exceptional value to their clients. For over two decades, these awards have been a benchmark for excellence, and we are honoured to be among the recipients.

## WHAT IT MEANS TO HEDGEFLOWS

Our co-founder, [Alex Axentiev](https://www.linkedin.com/in/axentiev/), expressed gratitude for this recognition, highlighting our team's unwavering dedication to supporting smaller businesses in navigating the complexities of international trade. This award is a testament to our relentless pursuit of innovation and our commitment to driving positive change within the industry.

One of the key reasons behind this accolade is our tireless efforts in developing a treasury management software solution tailored specifically to the unique needs and challenges faced by smaller businesses engaged in cross-border trade. With our user-friendly tools and comprehensive risk management features, we empower businesses to expand their global footprint while effectively mitigating risks.

Co-founder [Neh Thaker](https://www.linkedin.com/in/neh-thaker/) echoed this sentiment, extending sincere gratitude to TMI for acknowledging our dedication to innovation. This award is a reflection of the expertise and dedication of our team, as well as the trust and support of our valued clients. We remain steadfast in our mission to empower businesses to thrive in the global marketplace.

## The recognition

This recognition reaffirms our position as a leader in international trade finance and underscores our commitment to advancing the interests of smaller businesses worldwide. We are proud to provide small, medium, and mid-market-sized businesses with access to sophisticated financial tools that were once reserved for larger enterprises.
